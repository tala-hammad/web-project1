Key Insights

Impact on Students'Learning & Motivation

Research from An-Najah National University indicates that constantly following news of the assault on Gaza significantly diminishes students' academic motivation. This "screens of suffering" effect leads to increased psychological distress and a decline in educational engagement.

Post-Traumatic Stress in Healthcare Workers

According to studies published in Scientific Reports (Nature), Palestinian nursing staff are experiencing high levels of PTSD symptoms due to the war. These psychological burdens directly impact their overall quality of life and their capacity to provide care under extreme pressure.

Data-Driven Psychological Insights

Utilizing data from the Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics (PCBS), we observe shifting patterns in community resilience.
While historical data showed improvements in coping mechanisms up to 2023, current national statistics are vital for identifying the most vulnerable demographic groups in need of urgent support.
